What is Retroactive?

Retroactive is an event that gives FREE tokens to early participants who have been supported the project during early days. It usually happens in many AMMs (Automated Market Maker) or Layer 3 projects. In order to earn, users have to do some specific actions (bounties) to meet the 'requirements' such as making transactions, interacting with features. However, there are cases that users just need to 'be there' on day 1 (pics below).

On September 17th, 2020, Uniswap rewarded its users with 400 UNI (~2000$) - igniting the Retroactive movements later on.

Retroactive, or Airdrop - is a wonderful marketing move to attract more users to the projects. It's also a 'thank-you letter' for early contributors to incentivize them (and to HODL).

Airdrop: is just a synonym of retroactive. There are some distributors who refer to this word as a "planned retroactive" campaign.

Wait, what do you mean by "planned"?

Yep, you heard me right. The majority of happened retroactives haven't been announced to the community until they actually happen.
